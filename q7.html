About Audi-Q7

The Q7 is a great package. It offers class leading features, a premium cabin and butch looks. And all that with an Audi badge makes it one of the best SUV you can have in this price point.



Audi-Q7 OverView

The Audi Q7 has 2 Diesel Engine and 4 Petrol Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 2967 cc and 4134 cc while the Petrol engine is 3597 cc and 1984 cc and 2967 cc and 4134 cc . It is available with the Automatic transmission. Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Q7 has a mileage of 7.6 to 14.75 kmpl. The Q7 is a 7 seater suv and has length of 5086mm, width of 1983mm and a wheelbase of 3002mm.



Personal OverView

ADVANTAGES

  • The Q7 is available with both petrol and diesel engine options, unlike some of its rivals, which are diesel- or petrol-only offerings
  • The Q7 is 7-seater, while majority of its rivals can seat only five
  • The Q7 gets a 12.3-inch all-digital instrument cluster, a feature that is missing in many of its rivals
  • The Q7’s cabin is a delightful place to be in. While the low-set dash results in a commanding driving position, the seats are comfortable too
  • With two row of seats down, the Q7 has a luggage carrying capacity of upto 2075 litres, which is a lot

DIS-ADVANTAGES

  • The third row seats have a limited legroom, making them fit only for children
  • The Q7’s steering is precise, but is light and lacks feedback
  • With all three rows of seats up, the Q7 has a luggage carrying capacity of only 295 litres. For perspective, the Baleno has a boot capacity of 339 litres
