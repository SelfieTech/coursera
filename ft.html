About Jaguar F-Type

The updated Jaguar F-Type has a sensual design with sleek lines and a coupe silhouette. It comes with multiple engine options and trim levels, so there’s an F-Type for everyone. Plus, the 5.0-litre V8 offers blistering performance. Sure, it isn’t as polished as the Porsche 911 or the Audi R8, but it sure makes for a good alternative to the Porsche Cayman 718 and the Boxster 718.
