About Audi-Q8

Audi Q8 price starts at ₹ 98.99 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 1.34 Crore. The price of Petrol version for Q8 ranges between ₹ 98.99 Lakh - ₹ 1.34 Crore.



Audi-Q8 OverView

Entering the game dominated by players like RR Sport, Porsche Cayenne and BMW X6, the all-new Q8 is Audi’s newest flagship offering. Introduced at an exorbitant price, the coupe-SUV offers all the bells and whistles one could expect from Ingolstadt and packs in an explosive performance too. And it could be considered a bargain compared to Lamborghini Urus with which the Q8 shares its underpinnings.
