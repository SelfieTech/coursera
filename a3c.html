About Audi-A3 Cabriolet

The Audi A3 cabriolet edition in India is retailed at price of Rs 47.98 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). Of course, the highlight of the exterior of A3 Cabriolet is it folding roof design which is well complemented by LED headlamps, Aluminium trims, and 16-inch alloy wheels. You do get automatic fabric hood roof design but without roof rails. Inside the cabin, the A3 Cabriolet offers a two-seat option with aluminium accent, 3D design inlays, and multifunctional Steering wheel with an option of a flat bottom. The car is available with a single 1.4-TFSI petrol engine capable of putting out 148 bhp along with 250 Newton-metre of max torque. This engine is mated to a 7-speed automatic dual clutch transmission driving the front wheels of the car. Audi A3 Cabriolet has got the likes of DC Avanti, Mini Cooper convertible and BMW Z4 in its crosshairs among others.



Audi-A3 Cabriolet OverView

With GST kicking in, prices of the Audi A3 Cabriolet have been revised. Mumbai: The price of the convertible now starts at Rs 48.52 lakh, as opposed to Rs 50.15 lakh pre-GST. That's a difference of Rs 1.63 lakh. Delhi: The price of the convertible now starts at Rs 48.52 lakh, as opposed to Rs 47.98 lakh pre-GST. That's an increase of Rs 54,000. Do note that prices for insurance are marginally higher, as they are taxed at 18 per cent instead of the earlier 15 per cent.
