About Maserati GranTurismo

Maserati launched three new car models in India Maserati Gran Turismo , Maserati Quattroporte and Maserati Gran Cabrio. These cars are well affixed with various features, falling in a price range of between Rs 1.2 to Rs 1.5 crore. The super cars from Maserati is capable to give tough fight to other super luxury cars like BMW, Mercedes Benz, Audi etc in this world's second largest Indian auto market. The super luxury Maserati Gran Turismo is packed with a 4.2L engine that produces maximum power of 405bhp at 7,100 rpm and top torque of 460 Nm (47 kgm) at 4,750 rpm and the car has jet speed of 178 mph. Meanwhile dimensional of the car are 4881 mm, 2056 mm and 1353 mm which make it superb in the segment.



Maserati GranTurismo OverView

The updated Maserati GranTurismo was introduced in India last year with revised styling and a Ferrari powertrain. Even the interior has been updated with modern screens and added features. But, after being on sale for so long, there’s no hiding the fact that Maserati GT is truly an old car.
