About Audi-S5

The Audi S5 Sportback with the turbocharged V6 petrol engine is priced at Rs 70.60 lakh. The Audi S5 Sportback gets the only petrol engine in the range - a 3-litre turbo charged V6 that makes 349 bhp of peak power and 500 Nm of peak torque. The S5 of course also gets the Quattro all-wheel-drive setup and gets from 0-100 kmph in a quick 4.7 seconds and on to a limited top speed of 250 kmph.



Audi-S5 OverView

The Audi S5 Sportback comes packed with features like a large sunroof (that does make the car feel airier) and various interior seat packages. The S5 Sportback also comes standard with the likes of the LCD instrument cluster display that replaces the standard gauges and a large infotainment system that is controlled by Audi's MMI or multimedia interface. The infotainment system is also equipped with the likes of satellite navigation and Apple CarPlay.
