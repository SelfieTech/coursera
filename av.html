About Lamborghini Aventador

The Lamborghini Aventador is available in a choice of three variants - the S, S Roadster and the SVJ. All three of these are powered by a 6.5-litre V12 engine, albeit in different states of tune ranging from 700PS to 770PS in the most powerful SVJ variant. The torque figure, on the other hand, varies from 690Nm to 720Nm, depending on the variant chosen. The transmission unit is a 7-speed automatic which sends power to all four wheels. Its top speed has been rated at 350kmph.



Lamborghini Aventador OverView

The Aventador has outlandish looks that are bound to grab anyone’s attention. This two-seater mid-engine layout supercar might not be able to give the comfort or good ergonomics like your daily driver car. However, this is a maniac that will leave you spell-bound with its brutal power and fast nature.
