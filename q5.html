About Audi-Q2

Audi Q5 Price: Audi Q5 price starts at ₹ 50.27 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 56.26 Lakh. The price of Diesel variant for Q5 ranges between ₹ 50.27 Lakh - ₹ 56.26 Lakh and the price of Petrol variant for Q5 ranges between ₹ 50.27 Lakh - ₹ 56.26 Lakh. Audi Q5 Versions: Q5 is available in 4 variants. All the variants are Automatic. Audi Q5 Colours: Q5 is offered in 5 colours: Mythos Black Metallic, Ibis White, Navarra Blue Metallic, Floret Silver Metallic and Monsoon Grey Metallic. However, some of these colours are available in specific versions. Audi Q5 Competitors: Q5 is competing against Porsche Macan, BMW X3, Volvo XC60, Mercedes-Benz GLC, Land Rover Range Rover Evoque, Jaguar F-Pace, Audi A6, Lexus NX and BMW X5.



Audi-Q2 OverView

This is the petrol version of the Q5 mid-sized luxury SUV, powered by a 248bhp 1,984cc four-cylinder petrol engine. The car was initially launched with a diesel variant only earlier this year, in January, which was also the first launch of the year for the manufacturer. Now, the carmaker has recently expanded its portfolio by adding this petrol trim as well. This one, too, comes in two trim levels - premium plus and technology.
