About Jaguar XJ

There is a lot to like about the Jaguar XJ L. It looks unique and is the full-fat British luxury sedan amongst the German triad. Inside the extravagant cabin, the front seat comfort is really good, overall build quality is solid and ride and handling balance is better than the rivals. But the XJ has been around for so long no that it feels a bit dated.



Jaguar XJ OverView

Jaguar has launched the XJ50 in India, a special edition variant to celebrate 50 years of its flagship sedan, the XJ. Just like the XJ, the XJ50 can only be had as a long wheelbase option here in India. It is priced at Rs 1.11 crore (ex-showroom India). Jaguar XJ Price and Variants: The Jaguar XJ is available in three variants in India - Premium Luxury, Portfolio and the XJ50. Prices start at Rs 1.03 crore and and goes all the way up to Rs 1.11 crore. All prices are ex-showroom India. Jaguar XJ Engine and Transmission: All variants of the Jaguar XJ are powered by a 3.0-litre turbocharged diesel V6 that churns out 306PS of power and 689Nm of peak torque. Power is channeled to the rear wheels through an 8-speed automatic transmission. Jaguar XJ Features: The Jaguar XJ comes loaded with features, including 18-way adjustable front seats with ventilated heating, cooling and massage function, a park assist feature, a 360 degree view camera, adaptable LED headlamps, four-zone climate control, and more. Jaguar XJ Rivals: The Jaguar XJ rivals the likes of Audi A8L, BMW 7-Series and Mercedes-Benz S-Class in India.
