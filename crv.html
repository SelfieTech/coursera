Honda CR-V OverView

The new CR-V has grown in size, is comfortable and offers more space for its occupants. It has its fair share of negatives like the petrol CVT powertrain isn't for the enthusiasts, a mediocre touch screen system and limited boot space for luggage of five adults. Nonetheless, the CR-V has managed to tick boxes for a family-oriented SUV with good looks, a premium interior and car-like driving manners.
