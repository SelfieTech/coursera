About Bentley Bentayga

British luxury car manufacturer Bentley has launched the Bentayga SUV, with prices starting at Rs 3.85 Crore. The Bentayga is the first SUV from the British marquee and they have made sure it stands out. The design is quintessentially Bentley with trademark elements such as the split circular headlamps, large mesh grille and subtle character lines. The interiors are equally bespoke, featuring a blend of exquisite handcrafted wood and leather.



Bentley Bentayga OverView

The Bentley Bentayga has 2 Petrol Engine on offer. The Petrol engine is 5950 cc and 3998 cc . It is available with the Automatic transmission. Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Bentayga has a mileage of 10.9 to 11.11 kmpl. The Bentayga is a 5 seater suv and has length of 5141mm, width of 2223mm and a wheelbase of 2992mm.
