About Ferrari GTC4Lusso

TBy comparison, the recently-discontinued GTC4Lusso is powered by a screaming 6.3-liter V12 with 681 hp at 8,000 rpm and 514 lb-ft (697 Nm) of torque at 5,750 rpm. The car uses both a four-wheel drive system and four-wheel steering and is able to hit 62 mph (100 km/h) in 3.4 seconds while continuing through to a 208 mph (335 km/h) top speed. It’s a remarkable performance car and yet, is still extremely comfortable to drive over long distances.
