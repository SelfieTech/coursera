About Rolls Royce Wraith

The philosophy of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars goes hand in hand with the high fashion industry. Celebrating this, the company has revealed its exclusive ‘Wraith - Inspired by Fashion' edition. This Wraith comes with exquisite and desirable materials enhancing the beauty of this already breathtaking car. Design inspiration comes from different colour palettes, materials and techniques familiar to the fashion industry. Two-tone exterior colour scheme of Andalucian White and Arctic White compliments the signature shoulder-line further accentuated by a new hand-applied feature-line in the selected highlight colour hinting towards the interior of the Wraith. Just like finest clothing, the beauty lines in the extreme attention to detail paid on this vehicle.



Rolls Royce Wraith OverView

The Wraith is Rolls Royce’s most powerful series-production car to be ever built. It offers incredible performance, unparalleled levels of luxury, and a unique coupe design. The ‘magic-carpet’ ride quality means it still isn’t a sharp handler, and the coupe design makes the rear seat uncomfortable.
