About Jaguar XE

If you look at it, the Jaguar XE breaks the domination of the German trio in its segment. With its recent update, which isn’t a generation change, the XE has a sharper appearance, a newer powertrain option, and an updated cabin while it retains its Leaping Cat persona.



Jaguar XE OverView

Jaguar has launched the facelifted XE in India at Rs 44.98 lakh. Jaguar XE Variants & Price: It is available in two variants: S and SE. They are priced between Rs 44.98 lakh and Rs 46.33 lakh (ex-showroom India). Jaguar XE Engines: The Jaguar XE is available with two engine options: a 2.0-litre diesel that makes 180PS of power and 430Nm of peak torque and a 2.0-litre petrol engine, which puts out 250PS and 365Nm. Both engines are mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. Jaguar XE Features: It is equipped with features such as six airbags, ABS with EBD, ISOFIX child seat anchors, front and rear parking sensors, hill start assist and traction control. Other features on offer include dual-zone climate control, panoramic sunroof, wireless charging, and a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system. Jaguar XE Rivals: The XE continues to rival the likes of the BMW 3 Series, Mercedes-Benz C-Class, and the Audi A4.
