About Audi-R8

Audi R8 Price: Audi R8 price starts at ₹ 2.30 Crore and goes upto ₹ 2.72 Crore. The price of Petrol variant for R8 ranges between ₹ 2.30 Crore - ₹ 2.72 Crore. Audi R8 Versions: R8 is available in 2 variants. Out of these 2 variants, 1 are Manual and 1 are Automatic. Audi R8 Colours: R8 is offered in 10 colours: Vegas Yellow, Ibis White, Dynamite Red, Floret Silver, Camouflage Green, Daytona Grey, Suzuka Grey, Mythos Black, Tango Red and Macaw Blue. However, some of these colours are available in specific versions. Audi R8 Competitors: R8 is competing against Mercedes-Benz AMG GT, BMW M8, Porsche 911, Maserati GranTurismo, Mercedes-Benz S-Coupe, Jaguar F-Type, Lamborghini Huracan, Aston Martin Vantage and Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 4-Door Coupe.
