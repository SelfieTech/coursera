About Honda City

The Honda City is an aspirational sedan in its segment and continues to take the fight to similarly-priced SUVs like the Kia Seltos and Hyundai Creta. The new-gen model is sportier-looking, spacious, offers more features and feels premium than the fourth-gen City, which will continue to be sold alongside this. It is also exciting to drive and generates an emotion which its rivals lack.
