About BMW 6-Series

BMW 6 Series GT price starts at ₹ 65.87 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 76.96 Lakh. The price of Petrol version for 6 Series GT is ₹ 65.87 Lakh and the price of Diesel version for 6 Series GT ranges between ₹ 66.47 Lakh - ₹ 76.96 Lakh.



BMW 6-Series OverView

The 6 Series GT is now available in the new 620d Luxury Line variant at a price tag of Rs 63.90 lakh (ex-showroom, pan-India). Read details here. BMW offers a diesel engine option (3.0-litre V6 265PS/260Nm), starting at Rs 68.90 lakh (ex-showroom India), with the 6-Series GT. The BMW 6-Series GT was launched at the 2018 Indian Auto Expo with a petrol engine (2.0-litre 258PS/400Nm), now priced at Rs 63.9 lakh (ex-showroom India). BMW has also introduced a new premium variant of the 6-Series petrol. The BMW 6-Series GT was the first one from the Bavarian manufacturer to feature a BSVI-compliant engine in the country. The BMW 6-Series Gran Turismo gets an ‘Active’ kidney grille flanked by double-barrel adaptive LED cornering headlamps. Move over to the side and you’ll find a drooping roofline along with swooping lines across the car. The rear features wraparound LED tail lamps and dual exhausts as well. Sharing a lot of design cues with the 5-Series, the 6GT is equipped with a heads-up display, panoramic sunroof, BMW display key and multiple driving modes, including Comfort+, Adaptive, Eco Pro and Sport mode.
